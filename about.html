Our Story

Family-Owned.
Trusted Since 1999.

Delgado's Auto Service has been a cornerstone of Chicago's automotive repair community since 1999. What started as a family operation committed to honest work has grown into one of the neighborhood's most trusted repair shops — and we've never lost sight of what made us successful: treating every customer like a neighbor.

Our goal has always been simple: offer the best auto services available at reasonable prices. We provide easy-to-understand estimates before beginning any work, so you always know what you're paying for and why.

From routine oil changes to complex engine overhauls, our certified technicians handle every job with the same level of care and expertise. We stand behind all of our work with a guarantee you can trust.
