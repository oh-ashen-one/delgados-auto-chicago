Oil Change Service

Quick & Thorough Oil Changes

Regular oil changes are the single most important maintenance you can do to extend your engine's life. At Delgado's, every oil change includes a thorough inspection — we check your fluid levels, filters, belts, and tire pressure so nothing slips through the cracks.

We recommend what your vehicle actually needs — whether that's conventional, synthetic blend, or full synthetic — and never upsell you on services you don't require.

What's Included

  • Drain and replace engine oil
  • Replace oil filter
  • Top off all fluids (coolant, brake, power steering, windshield)
  • Inspect air filter and cabin filter
  • Check tire pressure and condition
  • Visual inspection of belts and hoses
  • Multi-point vehicle inspection
