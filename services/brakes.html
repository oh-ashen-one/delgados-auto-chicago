Brake Service

Chicago Brake Repair Experts

Your brakes are the most critical safety system on your vehicle. At Delgado's Auto Service, our ASE-certified technicians perform thorough brake inspections and repairs using quality parts that meet or exceed OEM specifications.

Brakes consist of two primary systems: hydraulics (brake fluid, master cylinder, calipers) and friction materials (pads and rotors). We inspect and service every component to ensure your vehicle stops reliably in Chicago traffic.

Signs You Need Brake Service

  • Squealing or grinding when braking
  • Vehicle pulls to one side when stopping
  • Brake pedal feels soft or spongy
  • Vibration in the steering wheel when braking
  • Warning light on dashboard

Our Brake Services

  • Complete brake system inspection
  • Brake pad replacement (all axles)
  • Rotor resurfacing and replacement
  • Caliper rebuild and replacement
  • Brake fluid flush and bleed
  • ABS system diagnostics
