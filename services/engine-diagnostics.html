Engine Services

Expert Engine Diagnosis & Repair

Modern vehicles have dozens of sensors constantly monitoring engine performance. When something goes wrong, your check engine light comes on — but that warning alone doesn't tell you what's broken. At Delgado's, we use professional-grade diagnostic equipment to read fault codes and pinpoint the exact problem.

We have access to Identifix, IATN, and Mitchell1 repair databases — the same tools used by dealerships — to ensure accurate diagnoses on every make and model.

  • Check engine light diagnosis
  • Engine tune-ups and spark plug replacement
  • Engine maintenance and oil service
  • Timing belt and chain service

  • Emissions diagnostics and repair
